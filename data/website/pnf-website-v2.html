Our Story

Born from a Bag Full
of Hope

The Priti Nourishment Foundation began with a simple but powerful idea: what if we could help families eat better without needing to read? In 2024, a group of passionate young people in Bagerhat, Bangladesh, set out to do exactly that.

Supported by GAIN and the SUN Youth Network Bangladesh, we launched the Hanging Nutrition Bag Project — eco-friendly bags with illustrated daily food menus, distributed to 25 low-income families. The bags used pictures, not words, so every household could follow along.

We believe nutrition is not only about food, but also dignity, awareness, and sustainable care.

That project became the foundation — and the inspiration — for everything PNF does today.

Hanging Nutrition Bag Project

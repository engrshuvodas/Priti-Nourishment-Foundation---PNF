The Origin

From a single Hanging Bag to a community revolution.

একটি পুষ্টি ব্যাগ থেকে একটি সামাজিক আন্দোলন।

Established in 2024, the Priti Nourishment Foundation (PNF) emerged as a response to the quiet crisis of malnutrition among the youth of Bagerhat. We realized that information, when packaged with dignity and innovation, has the power to transform lives.

Our "Hanging Nutrition Bag" wasn't just a tool; it was a symbol of youth stepping up to lead where traditional systems had limitations. Today, we are a vibrant network of students, activists, and health professionals dedicated to a healthier Bangladesh.
